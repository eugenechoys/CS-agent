# About CHOYS

## Mission & Vision
- Mission: Empowering every employee to thrive through personalized wellbeing, insurance, and rewards
- Core purpose: Provide every employee with access to benefits and impacts that suit their personal needs

## Founding Story
CHOYS was founded in 2020 by Sharon Li and Vanessa Chen in Singapore with a mission to make employee wellbeing accessible and personal for every worker in Asia.
