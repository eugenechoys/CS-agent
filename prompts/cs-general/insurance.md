# CHOYS Insurance Products

## Group Insurance Plans

CHOYS partners with leading insurance providers (AIA, Prudential, Great Eastern) to offer comprehensive group insurance coverage to employees.

### Group Medical Insurance
- Covers hospitalization, surgery, and specialist visits
- Coverage amounts vary by plan tier (Standard: $300K–$500K, Premier: $750K+)
- Copay typically ranges from $15–$30 depending on plan
- Covers pre- and post-hospitalization expenses
- Includes coverage for dependents (spouse and children)

### Group Dental Insurance
- Covers routine dental checkups, cleanings, and basic procedures
- Coverage amounts typically range from $1,500–$3,500 per year
- Copay ranges from $10–$15 per visit
- Plans available: Dental Basic, Dental Essential, Dental Plus

### Group Outpatient Insurance
- Covers GP visits, specialist consultations, and prescribed medications
- Coverage amounts range from $10,000–$30,000 per year
- Copay ranges from $20–$35 per visit
- Includes Virtual GP services through the CHOYS app

### Vision Insurance (starting Q2 2026)
- Vision insurance plans available from Q2 2026
- Covers eye exams, prescription lenses, frames, and contact lenses
- Coverage up to $1,500 annually
- Specific plan details and copay amounts may vary by employer plan design

## How to Check Your Coverage
1. Log in to the CHOYS app or employer portal
2. Navigate to "My Benefits" or "Insurance" section
3. View your plan details, coverage limits, and remaining balance
4. You can also ask the CHOYS support agent about your specific coverage

## How to File a Claim
1. Collect all relevant receipts and medical documents
2. Log in to the CHOYS app
3. Go to "Claims" section
4. Upload your documents and fill in the claim details
5. Submit the claim for processing
6. Claims are typically processed within 3–5 business days
7. For urgent claims or issues, contact help@getchoys.com

## Adding or Removing Dependents
- Dependents can be added during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth of child, etc.)
- Contact your HR department or email help@getchoys.com to initiate the process
- Required documents: marriage certificate, birth certificate, or other qualifying documentation

## Virtual GP Services
Available through the CHOYS app for all employees with outpatient coverage. You can consult a doctor remotely, receive prescriptions, and get medical certificates without visiting a clinic.
