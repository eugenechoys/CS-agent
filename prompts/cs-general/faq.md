# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Login

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox. If you don't receive an email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact help@getchoys.com.

**Q: How do I update my personal information?**
A: Open the CHOYS app, go to Settings > Profile, and update your details. Some fields (like department or role) may need to be updated by your HR admin.

**Q: I can't log in to the app. What should I do?**
A: First, make sure you're using the correct email address registered with your company. Try resetting your password. If the issue persists, contact help@getchoys.com with your company name and registered email.

## Benefits & Insurance

**Q: How do I check my insurance coverage?**
A: You can ask me directly! Just say "What are my insurance plans?" and I'll look up your coverage details. You can also check in the CHOYS app under "My Benefits."

**Q: When is open enrollment?**
A: Open enrollment periods are set by your company's HR department. Typically, open enrollment happens once a year. Contact your HR team for the exact dates for your company.

**Q: How do I add a dependent to my insurance?**
A: You can add dependents during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, adoption). Contact your HR department or email help@getchoys.com with the required documentation.

**Q: Can I change my insurance plan mid-year?**
A: No, changes can only be made during open enrollment unless you have a qualifying life event.

**Q: What counts as a qualifying life event?**
A: Marriage, divorce, birth of a child, adoption, or loss of other coverage.

**Q: How do I contact my insurance provider directly?**
A: Check your plan details in the CHOYS app for your provider contact info, or email help@getchoys.com for help.

**Q: How long does it take to process a claim?**
A: Claims are typically processed within 5–10 business days. You'll receive a notification in the app once your claim status is updated. For urgent inquiries, email help@getchoys.com.

## Wellness Programs

**Q: How do I join a wellness challenge?**
A: Open the CHOYS app, go to the "Challenges" or "Programs" section, and tap "Join" on any active challenge. Some challenges may be auto-enrolled by your HR team.

**Q: How do I earn wellness credits?**
A: You earn wellness credits by participating in wellness activities, completing challenges, and engaging with programs. Credits can be used for health-related purchases through the CHOYS rewards marketplace.

**Q: How do I use my wellness credits?**
A: Open the CHOYS app and go to the "Rewards" or "Marketplace" section to see your available wellness credit balance and eligible items. Select the item or service you want, apply your credits at checkout, and confirm the redemption. Some companies may have specific rules on what credits can be used for, so check your company’s benefits guidelines or ask your HR team if you’re unsure.

**Q: Do my wellness credits expire?**
A: Wellness credits expire at the end of each calendar year. Unused credits do not roll over. Check your balance in the app and use them before December 31.

**Q: What is the Employee Assistance Program (EAP)?**
A: EAP is a confidential counseling service available to all enrolled employees. It provides support for personal and work-related issues including stress, anxiety, and work-life balance. Sessions are free and confidential.

**Q: Is my EAP session confidential?**
A: Yes, absolutely. EAP sessions are completely confidential. Your employer will not know if you use the service or what you discuss. The only exception is if there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.

## Technical Support

**Q: The app is not loading or crashing. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps: (1) Close and reopen the app, (2) Check your internet connection, (3) Update the app to the latest version, (4) Restart your phone. If the issue continues, contact help@getchoys.com.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. CHOYS is ISO 27001 certified, which means we follow international standards for information security management. Your personal and health data is encrypted and protected.
