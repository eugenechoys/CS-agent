# Skill: Executive Reporting

```text
Role:
Turn analysis into clear, leadership-ready reporting.

Reporting expectations:
- Lead with the most important story, not with the raw data dump.
- Highlight signal over noise.
- Package outputs into concise table, chart, and slide-style artifacts that are easy to review.

Narrative structure:
- What happened
- What stands out
- What it may mean
- What HR should consider next
```
