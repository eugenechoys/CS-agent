# CHOYS App Troubleshooting Guide

This guide covers common issues members may face with the CHOYS app and suggested first-line fixes that CS agents can share.

---

## 1. App crashes on startup

**Symptoms**  
- App closes immediately after opening  
- Stuck on splash screen and then disappears

**Suggested steps for CS agents to share:**
1. Ask the member to force close the app and try again.
2. If it still crashes:
   - Ask them to **clear the app cache** (if available on their device/OS).
   - Then ask them to **delete/uninstall the app**.
   - Have them **reinstall the latest version** of the CHOYS app from the App Store / Google Play Store.
3. If the issue persists after reinstalling, collect:
   - Phone model and OS version  
   - App version  
   - Screenshots or screen recording (if possible)  
   - Time and date the issue occurred
4. Escalate to the appropriate internal tech team with these details.

---

## 2. Step counter not syncing

**Symptoms**  
- Steps in CHOYS do not match steps in Google Fit or Apple Health  
- No new steps appearing for the current day

**Suggested steps for CS agents to share:**
1. Confirm which platform the member is using:
   - **Android**: Google Fit  
   - **iOS**: Apple Health
2. Ask the member to check that CHOYS has the correct **permissions**:
   - Open **Google Fit / Apple Health**.  
   - Ensure CHOYS is allowed to **read steps / activity data**.
3. Ask the member to **disconnect and reconnect**:
   - In the CHOYS app, disconnect Google Fit / Apple Health (if possible in current build).  
   - Reconnect and ensure all requested permissions are granted.
4. Ask the member to **force close and reopen** the CHOYS app and wait a few minutes for data to sync.
5. If steps are still not syncing, collect:
   - Device type (Android/iOS), OS version  
   - Which tracker is used (Google Fit / Apple Health / others, if relevant)  
   - Screenshots of step count in CHOYS vs Google Fit / Apple Health  
   - Time when they noticed the issue
6. Escalate to the internal team with these details if unresolved.

---

## 3. Push notifications not working

**Symptoms**  
- Member does not receive reminders, challenges, or general notifications from CHOYS.

**Suggested steps for CS agents to share:**
1. Ask the member to check **phone settings**:
   - Go to the device **Settings > Notifications**.  
   - Find the **CHOYS** app.  
   - Ensure notifications are **allowed**, and alerts/banners, sounds, and badges are enabled as desired.
2. Ask the member to check **in-app notification settings** (if available):
   - Open the CHOYS app.  
   - Go to **Profile / Settings > Notifications**.  
   - Ensure relevant notification types are turned **on**.
3. Ask the member to ensure they have a **stable internet connection** (Wi‑Fi or mobile data).
4. Ask the member to **log out and log back in** to refresh their session.
5. If notifications are still not working, collect:
   - Device type and OS version  
   - App version  
   - Specific notifications missing (e.g., daily reminders, campaign notifications)  
   - Approximate date/time when they expected a notification
6. Escalate to internal team with these details if still unresolved.

---

## 4. Cannot see my insurance card

**Symptoms**  
- Insurance card section is blank or not loading  
- Member expects to see their digital insurance card but cannot find it

**Suggested steps for CS agents to share:**
1. Ask the member to go to the **Insurance / My Card** section in the CHOYS app.
2. Ask them to **pull down to refresh** the screen and wait a few seconds.
3. If still not visible, ask the member to:
   - **Log out** of the CHOYS app.  
   - **Log back in** and return to the insurance card section.
4. Confirm:
   - The member’s **email / identifier** used for login.  
   - Their **company / plan** (if applicable) to verify eligibility in backend systems.
5. If the card still doesn’t appear:
   - Check internal systems to confirm that the member’s insurance coverage is active and correctly linked to their CHOYS account.  
   - If any mismatch is found, follow existing internal process to correct mapping.
6. If issue persists after verification and refresh, escalate to internal tech/ops team with:
   - Member details (name, email, company)  
   - Screenshots of what the member sees in the app  
   - Time and date of the latest attempt.

---

*Note to CS agents: This guide is for first-line troubleshooting. If at any step you are unsure or the behavior looks unusual (e.g., affecting many users at once), escalate to the internal team and tag as a potential incident.*
