## Troubleshooting Guide

### Points Not Showing After Challenge
- Wait up to 24 hours for the app and rewards system to fully sync.
- After 24 hours, go to the Rewards page and perform a pull-to-refresh to update your points balance.
- If points are still missing after refresh, email **help@getchoys.com**.
