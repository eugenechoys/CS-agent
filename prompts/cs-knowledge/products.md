# CHOYS Products & Features

## CHOYS Platform Overview
CHOYS is an employee wellbeing, insurance, and rewards platform headquartered in Singapore. It provides next-gen AI-powered participation ops for automating employee programs. The platform is ISO 27001 certified and trusted by 20+ regional companies including Antler, Kopi Kenangan, and Kitabisa.

#### CHOYS Steps Challenges
CHOYS Steps Challenges motivate and reward employees to stay fit and active through friendly competitions.

With CHOYS Step Challenges, employees can join monthly and weekend competitions that focus on increasing daily step counts and overall activity levels.

**How to Enable Steps Tracking for Android**
- Install the Google Fit app.
- Log into Google Fit with the same email address used for the Google Play Store.
- Grant CHOYS access to your Google Fit step data so steps can be tracked for rewards.

**How to Enable Steps Tracking for iOS**
- Open the Apple Health app.
- Grant the CHOYS app permission to access your step data in Apple Health.

**How Step Challenges Work**
1. **Step Up Your Activity**  
   Step Challenges are designed to help employees prioritise physical wellbeing. Each challenge sets a step target, turning daily movement into an enjoyable and rewarding habit.

2. **Monthly and Weekend Challenges**  
   Employees can choose between longer monthly challenges and shorter weekend challenges, depending on their schedule and preferences.

3. **Health and Teamwork**  
   Challenges can be team-based, encouraging collaboration, mutual support, and celebration of milestones.

4. **Elevate Your Wellbeing**  
   By joining Step Challenges, employees can build healthier habits, enjoy friendly competition, and earn CHOYSCoins as part of their wellbeing journey.

### CHOYS Mood Tracker
The CHOYS Mood Tracker is a daily mood log that helps employees track their long-term emotional wellbeing.

At CHOYS, we believe that being in tune with your feelings is essential for your overall wellbeing. The Mood Tracker is designed to make emotional self-awareness simple, secure, and rewarding.

**Key Benefits**

1. **Simple and Rewarding**  
   - One-tap daily mood logging.  
   - Earn CHOYSCoins for consistently recording moods.  
   - Use CHOYSCoins to support meaningful social causes through donations.

2. **Insights Into Your Wellbeing**  
   - Wellbeing reports that highlight mood patterns over time.  
   - Help employees identify trends, triggers, and factors influencing their emotional state.

3. **Nurture Emotional Health**  
   - Provides a safe space to reflect on feelings.  
   - Supports emotional awareness, resilience, and personal growth.

4. **Secure and Private**  
   - Mood data is kept secure and confidential.  
   - Employees have full control over their data and can choose whether to keep reports private or share them with trusted individuals.

Keeping a daily record of mood is one step towards better emotional awareness and mental wellbeing. The CHOYSCoins earned through Mood Tracker activities can be donated to meaningful social causes, letting employees contribute positively while caring for their mental health.
